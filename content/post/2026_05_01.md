Today, we welcome two new members in the uOttawa Geophysics Group: Finley St-Denis and Sarah Pelchat. Both obtained their BSc in Geology-Physics at the University of Ottawa, and will work on characterizing the seismic velocity structure of the Hikurangi subduction zone using the ELVES data.

Stay tuned for updates on their research!
